Instructions

You will be given a magic number at the start of each game.

There are four crystals. By clicking on the crystal you will add a specific amount to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score to the magic number. If your score goes over the magic number, you lose that round.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

In each new round the magic number and the value of each crystal will change.
